# EWRL1 (1994)

First European Workshop on Reinforcement Learning (EWRL 1994)


Dates:    27-28 September 1994
Location:  IRIDIA, ULB, Brussels, Belgium, European Union

## Tuesday 27 September 1994
10:00 	Introductory remarks
10:15 	Reinforcement learning: An overview of current research directions – Richard Sutton and Chris Watkins
11:15 	IRIDIA research in reinforcement learning – Hugues Bersini
12:15 	Lunch (not included in the organization)
14:00 	Ant-Q: Reinforcement learning in combinatorial optimization – Marco Dorigo
15:00 	Reinforcement learning for 2-player games strategies – Antonella Carbonaro
16:00 	Coffee break
16:30 	Fuzzy control and Q-learning – Ann Nowe’
17:30 	Fuzzy Q-learning and evolutionary strategies for adaptive fuzzy control – Pierre-Yves Glorennec
19:00 	Social dinner (organized by EWRL-1)

##  Wednesday 28 September 1994
10:00 	Q-hat-learning – Matthias Heger
11:00 	Co-operation of multiple agents through filtered payoff – Sadayoshi Mikami
12:00 	Lunch at the ULB students restaurant
14:00 	Exploration versus Exploitation: total and limited rationality – Nicolas Meuleau
15:00 	Reinforcement learning for fuzzy rules: the ELF approach – Filippo Basso
15:30 	Final discussion
